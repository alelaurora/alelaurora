Client Dashboard

BTG Pactual

BTG Pactual Client Dashboard shows a summary of your account, investments, a virtual wallet and keeps track of your movements. I've worked to adapt the experience from desktop to tablets and give design consultancy for opportunities and constraints on site's Visual Design. On Jan 2016, joined FJORD São Paulo to launch the new digital transformation.

For this project, I’ve worked on several parts, some wireframes, gird, navigation, icons, and a lot of responsive breakpoints, solving problems that came up with the small amount of space, and large amount of data, on different devices.
