Design Ecosystem

La Segunda is the 4th insurance company in Argentina. B I O M A is La Segunda's design system for digital products.

Inspired with their cooperative and sustainability brand values, I name it Bioma (spanish for biome). It suggests a flexible, growing, and ever-changing system collaboration, in which, the design system will grow with different platforms, and they will grow with it.

B I O M A

La Segunda

When I entered the company on 2019, it was going through a very rapid expansion of software development. The B I O M A was created in order to unify interfaces, company's voice and tone, consolidate experiences and scale quickly.

It was a large process that started 2 years ago with some colors, type, buttons and input forms, growing from platforms that were needed.

It's also supported in a website, based on bootstrap in order to provide working code for developers and speed up the time to market.

Since it's creation has been applied in more than 20 different platforms.
