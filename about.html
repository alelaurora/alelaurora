About Me

Born in 1986 in Rosario, Argentina. I've been transforming ideas into digital products for more than 8 years.

Over the last 6 years I've been working as a Senior UX Designer for big clients.

Since the last year, after working as main UX Designer of the Digital Transformation team and as Scrum Master in different projects, I was able to put together a team of designers, developing myself as UX Design Team Leader and establish the UX Design throughout the company as a process.
