About Me

I'm a Product Designer that loves to connect with people through design. I prefer to see things not as they are but as they could be, share my ideas and vision, and take the necessary actions to create enjoyable and seamless experiences.

I have more than 8 years of experience designing, leading, and collaborating with other teams. Currently, I'm working as UX Team Leader at 'La Segunda Seguros' in Rosario, Argentina.

My main skills are:

  • Provide feedback to the design team.
  • Promote the creation and development of design patterns.
  • Keep an eye on consistency and accessibility.
  • Find out potential issues or try to solve them with the team.
  • Work closely with the whole team, from the stakeholders to development teams.
  • User-centric focus.
  • Over-communicate, to avoid misunderstandings and make sure that the team is aligned.
  • Keep close attention to details
  • Strong Scrum framework knowledge.
  • Simplicity over complexity & function over form.
  • Tool agnostic - I can manage a wide range of tools or quickly learn new ones.
  • Proficiency in English, both oral and written.
